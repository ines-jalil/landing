About us

Who said cars couldn't be reconditioned? Renaissance offers a unique custom car sales experience, where each vehicle is transformed into a masterpiece, promising passion and history. We buy and restore old cars, turning them into personalized works of art, blending tradition and innovation. Our team of passionate experts brings them back to life, giving them a new aesthetic and optimal performance.

Embark on a true automotive Renaissance with us, and let us drive you towards the creation of your dream car.

Buying history...

At the heart of sustainability challenges lie both environmental and human issues. At Renaissance, we believe that the answer is not only to be found in the future, but rather in its articulation with the past. We believe that everything deserves a second chance, and that every car deserves a second life.

So whether it's a vehicle from another era or a relatively recent model, as long as it has already written its history with yours, Renaissance offers to buy it back from you, and transform it so that it can continue to make someone else's eyes shine.

... to share dreams

More than a car, we offer you your car, tailored and reconditioned to perfectly match your needs. We offer you premium service as well as a personalized experience. Let your heart and creativity express themselves as you design the curves of a true work of art.

With just a few clicks, you can choose to give a vehicle a new life, thanks to restoration work that emphasizes attention to detail. And ultimately, during this unique automotive Renaissance, let your dreams take over.
